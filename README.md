# Service-to-Sales Audit Platform

Data-driven service-to-sales audit platform for RV dealerships. Identifies hidden pipeline opportunities from service department records.

## Overview

This platform transforms underutilized service department data into actionable sales leads for RV dealerships. By analyzing service records, we identify trade-in candidates, expiring warranties, and missed upsell opportunities.

## Key Features

- **Trade-In Identification**: Identify customers with aging RVs or high maintenance costs
- **Warranty Expiration Tracking**: Reach customers before coverage ends with renewal offers
- **Upsell Opportunities**: Discover cross-sell and accessory opportunities from past service visits
- **Data Integration**: Seamless integration with major DMS platforms (IDS, Lightspeed, DealerRock)

## Value Proposition

- Typical audit uncovers **$100K+ in pipeline value**
- **20x ROI** on $5,000 one-time audit engagement
- Target market: **8,500+ RV dealerships** in US
